Cafe Aylanto - Reservation & Takeaway Policy

RESERVATIONS:
- Advance table booking is strongly recommended, especially for weekends and evenings.
- Reservations can be made for parties of 2 up to large group celebrations.
- A valid contact number is required to confirm any reservation.
- For special occasions (birthdays, anniversaries), please mention this at time of booking.

TAKEAWAY ORDERS:
- Takeaway ("take-out") orders are prepared fresh and typically take 30-40 minutes given the fine-dining menu.
- Both credit card and cash payments are accepted.
- Orders can also be placed through delivery partners, but phone/voice orders are handled directly by the restaurant.

GENERAL:
- Cafe Aylanto is a fine-dining restaurant; smart casual dress is recommended for dine-in.
- Kid-friendly seating and wheelchair accessibility are available.
- Valet parking is offered during busy hours.
