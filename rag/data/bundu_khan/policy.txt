Bundu Khan - Reservation & Takeaway Policy

RESERVATIONS:
- Reservations require at least 2 hours advance notice.
- Maximum party size for a standard reservation is 10 guests. Larger groups need manager approval.
- Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the booked time before the table may be released.
- No deposit required for parties under 10.

TAKEAWAY ORDERS:
- Takeaway orders are typically ready for pickup within 20-25 minutes.
- Full payment is required at the time of order confirmation.
- Cash and card payments are both accepted for takeaway.
- Orders cannot be modified once confirmed and sent to the kitchen.

GENERAL:
- Breakfast items are only available until 12:00 PM.
- The restaurant is family-friendly and accommodates walk-ins alongside reservations.
